What We Do

Creative. Technical.
Executed.

From concept to completion — we bring together artistic vision and technical mastery to deliver audiovisual experiences at any scale.

▩

AV Installation

Permanent audiovisual systems for restaurants, bars, corporate spaces, and venues. Sound design, speaker placement, display integration, and acoustic treatment tailored to your space.

▷

Event Production

End-to-end live event audio and visual production. From intimate performances to large-scale festivals — sound reinforcement, stage design, lighting coordination, and live mixing.

◉

Studio Design

Recording studio design, setup, and optimization. Acoustic treatment, equipment selection, signal flow architecture, and workflow design for artists and producers.

◦

Consulting

Strategic project management and creative direction for audiovisual projects. Vendor coordination, budget planning, timeline management, and technical specification.

♫

Music Production

Electronic and live music production, mixing, and sound design. From composition to final master — working with synths, guitars, pedals, DAWs, and hybrid setups to bring your sonic vision to life.
